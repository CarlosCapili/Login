Nate Robinson

The man who got knocked out by Jake Paul

Here's a timeline of Nate Robinson's life before the disaster

  • 1984 - Born in Seattle, Washington
  • 2005 - NBA draft 1st round pick 21 selected by the Phoenix Suns, traded to the New York Knicks
  • 2006 - 1st NBA Slam Dunk Contest Champion
  • 2009 - 2nd NBA Slam Dunk Contest Champion
  • 2010 - 3rd NBA Slam Dunk Contest Champion, traded to the Boston Celtics
  • 2011 - Traded to the Oklahoma City Thunder
  • 2012 - Signed with the Golden State Warriors
  • 2013 - Signed with the Chicago Bulls
  • 2014 - Signed with the Denver Nuggets
  • 2015 - Played with the LA Clippers and New Orleans Pelicans before being wavied
  • 2019 - Nate calls out Jake Paul to a boxing match
  • 2020 - Knocked out cold at the age of 36
